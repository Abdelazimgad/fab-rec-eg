Our Story and Mission

FAB-REC is a pioneering Egyptian organization that combines environmental protection with empowering women breadwinners.

The idea started from a simple vision: turning textile waste from an environmental problem into an economic opportunity.

We work with women from underserved communities, train them in sewing and sustainable design, and showcase their products in the trainee marketplace.

Every purchase supports a woman and reduces waste.
